About

I'm a Tennessee Tech Undergraduate (about to graduate in DECEMBER 2025) I've been working as a Computer Science major with a concentration in Cybersecurity for a while now and I've been trying to understand more about how the world interacts with my professional interests (that being a mix of networking, software development, and cybersecurity). Over time, I grew a bit bored of it which may be due to school. Nowadays, my goals are to both code for myself, and enjoy myself whilst I learn

Now, I want to be able to contribute more towards the field than I already have as both a CEROC Ambassador and as an AI-Cyber Researcher. Both jobs offered a lot in the ways of giving me opportunities and I think I'll be able to put forward my best my next roles because of them.

Many programmers go through what is called the Overjustification Effect as a result of their job, making them lose passion for this space. This website is my answer to that, and I will continue to post what I love to it.
